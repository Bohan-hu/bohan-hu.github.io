Bohan Hu is a third-year Ph.D. student from Microelectronics Thrust, Function Hub, The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou).

He focuses on the architecture design and compilation techniques for electronic-photonic accelerators.

His research interests lie at the intersection of computer architecture and photonics, aiming to enhance computational efficiency and performance through co-designed hardware and software solutions.

Research Interests

  • HW/SW co-design for photonic computing

  • Machine learning compilers
